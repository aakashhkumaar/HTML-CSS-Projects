Blue Lock's Ace

Yoichi Isagi is the main protagonist of the manga and anime series Blue Lock. He is a second-year student at Ichinan High School who was a player for the school's football team. After losing a match and arriving at the Blue Lock facility like all the other 300 participants and became a member of Team Z for the First Selection. He's main goal is to become the world's best striker.
